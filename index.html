What is Curtailment?

Curtailment is about balancing energy supply and demand. During periods of excessive solar or wind energy production, where supply exceeds demand, the energy grid can become unbalanced, resulting in negative energy prices and costs for returning excess energy. To prevent this, producers of green energy, such as wind turbines and solar farms, can temporarily reduce their production.

Our Role

MyEnergyCY is a pioneer in the application of curtailment. We predict the real-time energy production of producers and, if necessary, apply curtailment to avoid energy feed-in.

With our advanced solutions, you can curtail your installation without hardware, and with our platform, you have a device that can also read energy meters and control other energy technologies.
